Yogurt Bowl

Description

This yogurt is packed with lots of protein and nutrients to fuel your next workout and give you a healthy and balanced midday snack.

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Berry Medley (I prefer the Frozen Three Berry Blend from Costco)
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Defrost the frozen berries in the microwave (Defrost Setting)
  2. Add Yourt
  3. Sprinkle Granola on top
  4. Drizzle Honey
  5. Enjoy!
